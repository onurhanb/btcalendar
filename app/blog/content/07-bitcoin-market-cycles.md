## Bitcoin Market Cycles

Bitcoin moves in cycles.

Historically:

- accumulation
- expansion
- euphoria
- correction

## Why calendars help

Charts show lines.

Calendars show behavior.

You can visually spot:

- months of strength
- prolonged weakness
- volatility spikes

## Long-term perspective

Investors benefit most from macro views.

Calendar layouts encourage zooming out.
